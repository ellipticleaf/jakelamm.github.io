Project
Brief

G Street Wheel is an interactive installation designed to bring a local community closer together. Built around the metaphor of a spinning wheel, the experience invites neighbors to contribute, connect, and discover what they share with the people around them.

The goal was to create something that felt alive — a digital and physical touchpoint where community members could leave their mark and see how their voices fit into the larger whole.

Designing for Connection

The
Approach

The design centers on participation — the wheel metaphor naturally draws people in, giving everyone an equal spoke in the experience. The interface was kept intentionally simple so that anyone, regardless of technical comfort, could engage with it immediately.

Interactions were layered to reward curiosity: the more a user explored, the more connections and contributions from their community revealed themselves, reinforcing the sense of shared space and belonging.
